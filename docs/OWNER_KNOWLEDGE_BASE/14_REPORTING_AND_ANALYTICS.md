## CUSTOMER_ANALYTICS

> **Verification Status:** `OWNER_DEFINED`
> **Date:** 2026-06-09

Customer analytics should include:

- Most purchased products
- Most purchased brands
- Average time between orders
- Last order
- Average order value
- Total purchases

## SALES_MANAGER_REPORTING

> **Verification Status:** `OWNER_DEFINED`
> **Date:** 2026-06-09

Sales Managers may view:

- Individual representative reports
- Team-wide reports

Within their own team only.

Sales Managers may access:

Detailed customer analysis for customers within team ownership scope.
